How to use this with an audience

Turn the briefing into a guided discovery session.

The facilitator should avoid explaining every detail upfront. Let the audience form a mental model, then use the interactive elements to surface the hard questions.

Suggested 12-minute flow

  1. Open with the three mismatches.
  2. Let the audience toggle the BA/TRL map.
  3. Use the farm metaphor to make the portfolio role intuitive.
  4. Show the fractal engine to explain project generation.
  5. Close with the scenario lab: “what PM time comes back?”

Audience takeaway

RDT&E PMs are not under-skilled. They are overburdened.

A simulation-driven portfolio model can accelerate AM institutionalization without increasing headcount by turning fragmented manual work into a living decision system. The PM shifts from reactive spreadsheet maintainer to proactive portfolio architect.
